Equipoise

Compliance workflow for the EU Pay Transparency Directive (2023/970). For HR teams using Greenhouse and Workday.

The directive transposition deadline is June 7, 2026.

Mercer's 2026 Global Pay Transparency Survey found that only 9% of Europe-based employers have a full transparency strategy.

Equipoise reads job postings and compensation data via your existing ATS, lints requisitions for directive compliance, computes Article 9 gender pay gap reports, and tracks member-state transposition deadlines across all 27 EU countries.

Launching Q2 2026.
